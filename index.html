Visit Saint Petersburg, Florida

Nicknamed "The Sunshine City" and features some of the nation's top ranked beaches.

Top three activities to do in Saint Petersburg

Soak up the sun at St.Pete Beach

St. Pete Beach is a perfect place to go when you want to relax and take in the beautful view.

Visit the Dali Museum

The Dali Museum houses comprehensive collection of paintings, with a total collection of more than 2,000 works of art

Head out to the new St.Pete Pier

The brand new pier is 26 acres of fun, food, activities and entertainment. The pier is an ideal place to spend the afternoon with family or an evening with a group of friends.
